My First UX Article
The Beginner's Problem

One of the most peculiar thing i have seen in my short yet interesting UX journey so far is that we often get overwhelmed by the Design trends and end up creating something that is far away from being practical in terms of development and scaling. I have come across many dashboards on behance that have great aesthetics but doesnt even have proper navigation and drill down which results in great UI but bad UX.

One of the standard practices of learning UX is to pick up an existing app and try to design better flows and functionalities that can solve current problems. This ofcourse will need a lot of user research. Hence picking up a popular app is always helpful. A strong user research is the key to a great user experience, as you can build great modern functionalities in your app or website but only a great research on user behaviour will tell you which is the most relevant one.
